Committee OS is a deterministic decision engine. It accepts a mission, processes data through structured logical stages, and produces distilled, actionable output. The intelligence lives in the architecture. Artificial intelligence is the finishing step — not the motor.

01 Product Overview

A decision engine that does not guess.

Every organisation processes a continuous flood of signals — content, code, customer voice, market data, security events. Most systems hand that flood to a language model and ask it to decide. Committee OS does not. It applies deterministic logic to every stage of that process, scoring and ranking inputs against a structured rubric before a single AI call is ever made.

The result is a system with structural integrity: predictable, auditable, and reproducible. When Committee OS surfaces a recommendation, that recommendation is traceable to a score, a criterion, a rule — not to the probabilistic output of a model left to its own devices.

02 Operating Principle

Seven stages. Six of them run without asking a model anything.

Data enters through a defined ingest channel and moves through a strict sequence of transformations. Each stage has a contract: a defined input, a defined output, and a defined failure behavior. No stage may pass ambiguous data to the next. No stage may infer what it cannot verify.

The AI worker at Stage 5 receives only structured, pre-scored data. It does not ingest raw content. It does not assign scores. It synthesises rankings that the deterministic pipeline has already established — and its output is validated against a hard contract before the pipeline proceeds.

03 Evaluation Framework

Eight lenses. Every unit scored against all of them — always.

The rubric is the engine. Every content unit that passes filtration is scored against eight independent evaluation lenses, each derived from a distinct professional domain. Scoring is computational: each criterion within a lens resolves to a binary signal. The lens score is a ratio. The aggregate score is a weighted mean. No lens is ever silenced; the operator may amplify up to three per session.

L-01
Creative Director
Originality, format signal, narrative potential, brand recognition
L-02
Financial Director
Cost implication, ROI signal, resource mention, expenditure risk
L-03

L-08

Risk flag, compliance signal, contradiction detection, content integrity

Lens weights live in configuration — not in prompts. Adjusting the evaluation emphasis of a deployment requires a configuration edit, not a conversation with a model.

06 System Integrity

What this system is not is as important as what it is.

The market is saturated with systems that substitute parallel AI calls for structured reasoning. Committee OS was designed explicitly in opposition to that pattern. The following are architectural violations — conditions that, if present in any implementation, render it non-compliant.

✕ A prompt chain — multiple open-ended AI calls substituting for deterministic logic
✕ Self-scoring — any AI model evaluating its own output at any stage
✕ Prompt-embedded thresholds — scores or weights defined inside a prompt string
✕ Silent gap-filling — any component inferring missing data rather than flagging it
✕ Scope creep — any AI worker performing tasks outside their defined stage
✕ Unvalidated output — any AI response passed downstream before contract validation

These constraints are not aspirational. They are enforced at the architectural level. The AI budget ceiling is a hard integer in configuration. Violating it halts the pipeline regardless of run state. No further AI calls are permitted in that run.

07 Deployment Position

Built for organisations where decisions have consequences.

Committee OS is not a feature. It is infrastructure. It is appropriate for any operational context where the volume of incoming signals exceeds the bandwidth of human attention, where the cost of a bad recommendation is real, and where traceability and auditability are not optional.

It runs in fourteen domains out of the box — from security operations to academic research synthesis, from financial intelligence to content production — and extends to new domains without modifying the core pipeline. It surfaces three ranked, validated recommendations per run, every time, with a full audit trail from raw input to final output.

"Most AI systems are powerful but unpredictable. Committee OS is the inverse proposition: a system whose structural integrity does not depend on the model — because the architecture was built to be the reliable one."
